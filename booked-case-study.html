The Problem

MANUAL WORKFLOWS. EXPENSIVE ALTERNATIVES.

Many talent teams are caught between fully manual scheduling workflows and enterprise tools that cost $30K–$60K per year. Coordinators cross-reference calendars by hand, build invites from scratch, and attach documents manually — for every single interview.

The gap between "spreadsheets and Google Forms" and "GoodTime enterprise contract" is exactly where Booked was built to live.

No scheduling infrastructure

Teams rely on generic forms and manual calendar checking, costing 10–20 minutes per interview in coordinator time.

Documents attached by hand

Interview guides emailed ad hoc with no consistency, no guarantee they reach the right people at the right time.

Zero visibility for leadership

No dashboard, no reporting, no weekly summary. Coordinator workload is completely invisible to department heads.

Expensive or mismatched alternatives

GoodTime runs $30K–$60K/yr. Calendly Enterprise is $10K–$20K/yr. Both are overbuilt for many TA teams.

The Solution

STRUCTURED. REPEATABLE. TA-OWNED.

Booked replaces the manual process with a structured 4-step intake workflow, a live coordinator dashboard, and automated reporting — at zero licensing cost, with full ownership by the TA team.

📋

4-Step Intake Form

Replaces ad hoc scheduling with a structured workflow — req ID, candidate info, interviewers, interview mode, and document attachment all in one place.

📊

Live Coordinator Dashboard

Today, This Week, and All Upcoming views. Every interview visible at a glance — mode, stage, interviewers, recruiter, and time all on one card.

📧

Automated Email Confirmation

Branded confirmation emails generated automatically with interview-specific tips, guide links, and what to expect — no manual drafting.

📈

Department & Recruiter Reporting

Summary and detailed reports by department, role, and recruiter. Printable for leadership in one click.

How It Was Built
