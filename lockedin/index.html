The problem

Scheduling is a full-time job. That's the problem.

On average, scheduling a single interview loop takes 45–90 minutes of coordinator time. Multiply that across 20+ open reqs and you've lost an entire workday — every week — just on logistics.

Most recruiting teams use 4–6 disconnected tools to schedule a single interview. Locked In. consolidates availability parsing, calendar invites, confirmations, and tracking into one workflow.

When a recruiter asks 'where are we on this candidate?' the answer usually involves opening 3 tabs and sending a Slack message. Locked In. gives recruiters a real-time dashboard so the answer is always one click away.

The solution

One platform. Two personas. Total control.

For Recruiters

  • Resume upload + ResumeGate
  • AI email drafting
  • Dashboard + reports
